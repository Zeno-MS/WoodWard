## III. The Procurement Blind Spot: Legal, Routine — and Structurally Invisible

Here is the mechanism, stripped of theater:

When parents hear “no bid,” they often assume wrongdoing. The more uncomfortable reality is worse in a different way: the system can be **lawful**, **routine**, and still produce outcomes that are hard for the public to evaluate as good stewardship.
