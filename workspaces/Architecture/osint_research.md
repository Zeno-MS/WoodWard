# OSINT Research: Amergis, Maxim, and Soliant

## Executive Summary
*   **Amergis Healthcare Staffing** is the new name for **Maxim Healthcare Staffing**. This is a direct rebranding, not just an officer overlap.
*   **Maxim Healthcare Services** settled for **$150 Million** in 2011 to resolve criminal and civil charges of Medicaid fraud.
*   **Soliant Health** is a separate entity also registered in WA.

## Detailed Findings

### 1. Amergis = Maxim
*   **Finding**: Amergis Healthcare Staffing, Inc. was formerly known as Maxim Healthcare Staffing.
*   **Headquarters**: Columbia, MD (Same as Maxim).
*   **WA Registration**: Registered in Washington State (UBI search confirmed).
*   **Implication**: Any "past bad acts" of Maxim are directly relevant to Amergis contracts.

### 2. Maxim Healthcare 2011 Fraud Settlement
*   **Date**: September 2011
*   **Amount**: **$150 Million** ($20M criminal fine, $130M civil penalties).
*   **Charges**: scheme to defraud Medicaid and VA programs (billing for services not rendered, falsified timesheets, unlicensed offices).
*   **Criminal Pleas**: 9 individuals pleaded guilty, including 8 former employees.
*   **Agreements**: Deferred Prosecution Agreement (DPA) and Corporate Integrity Agreement (CIA) imposed.
*   **Relevance**: This establishes a history of systemic fraud involving billing and timesheets—exactly the risk factor for "uncapped" hourly vendor contracts.

### 3. Soliant Health
*   **Status**: Active registration in WA.
*   **Role**: Competitor/Peer to Amergis in the VPS ecosystem.
