## Maxim Healthcare — 2011 Medicaid Fraud Settlement

**Confirmed via FBI, DOJ, CBS News, and ProPublica:**

- **Settlement Amount:** ~$150 million total
  - $20 million criminal penalty
  - ~$130 million civil settlements (~$70M federal, ~$60M to 42 states)
- **Admission:** Defrauded Medicaid and VA programs of $61+ million
- **Scheme:** False bills for services not provided, not documented, or rendered by unlicensed offices
- **Individuals Charged:** 9 (including former employees and senior managers)
- **Outcome:** Deferred Prosecution Agreement (DPA) — avoided conviction with compliance reforms

**Sources:**
- [FBI Archive](https://www.fbi.gov/)
- [DOJ Press Release](https://www.justice.gov/)
- [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/)
- [ProPublica](https://www.propublica.org/)
