## 1. Central Office Reductions (FTE & Duty Roots)

*   **Pre-cut Total Central Office FTE (2023-24):** 480.63 FTE
*   **Post-cut Total Central Office FTE (2024-25):** 433.06 FTE

*   **Duty Root 92** (Instructional Support): -12.10 FTE
*   **Duty Root 96** (Family/Community): -9.15 FTE
*   **Duty Root 13** (Other District Admin): -5.13 FTE
*   **Duty Root 64** (Maintenance): -2.60 FTE
*   **Duty Root 95** (Operations): -2.34 FTE
*   *Note: These reductions sum to slightly more than the net 47.57 cut because some duty roots (like root 21) saw minor FTE increases.*

**Analysis:** The claim of "19-20" central office reductions might refer to a specific subset of administrators (e.g., Duty Root 11-13 which saw a ~10 FTE drop) rather than the raw S-275 data for the entire building. The total FTE reduction at the district office was actually larger (47.57 FTE), but heavily concentrated in support personnel (Root 40), systemic support (Roots 92/97), rather than executive administration.

## 2. TOSA Classification Claim
The S-275 coding for Teachers on Special Assignment (TOSAs) housed at the central office is as follows:
*   **Location Code (Building Number):** 1014 (District Office)
*   **Duty Root:** 33 ("Other Teacher")

In 2023-2024, there were 10 unique individuals coded as Duty Root 33 at Location 1014, representing roughly 9.53 FTE. By 2024-2025, this was reduced to 9 individuals. Because TOSAs are coded under instructional duty roots (30-series) rather than administrative roots (10- or 20-series), they are officially classified as instructional staff despite being assigned to the central office.
