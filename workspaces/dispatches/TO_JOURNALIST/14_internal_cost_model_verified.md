## 1. Executive Summary
We have completed the **Internal Cost Model** to quantify the specific financial toll of the district's shift toward staffing agencies. By extracting base salaries from the 2024-2025 VEA and VAESP collective bargaining agreements and adding real-world overhead (SEBB health insurance, DRS pensions, FICA, etc.), we established the "Fully Loaded" cost of a district employee.

**The Finding:** The district is bleeding massive amounts of capital to agency overhead. Even when factoring in Washington's generous pensions and healthcare, agency contractors cost between **24% and 117% more** per hour than direct hires.
