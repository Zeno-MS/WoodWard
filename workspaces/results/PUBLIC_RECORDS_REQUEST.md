Pursuant to the Washington Public Records Act (RCW 42.56), I am requesting access to and copies of the following public records maintained by Vancouver School District No. 37. I request that responsive documents be provided in **electronic format** (PDF, Excel, or CSV) where available, delivered via email or secure file transfer.

---

## Request 1: Staffing Agency Contracts and Agreements

All current and historical **Master Service Agreements (MSAs)**, Statements of Work, rate schedules, contract amendments, and renewal notices between Vancouver School District No. 37 and the following entities, covering the period **July 1, 2019 through the present**:

- Maxim Healthcare Services, Inc.
- Amergis Healthcare Staffing (formerly Maxim Healthcare Staffing)
- Soliant Health, LLC
- Any successor, subsidiary, or affiliated entity of the above

This request includes but is not limited to:

- Bill rates and markup percentages by role category (e.g., Speech-Language Pathologist, Registered Nurse, Paraeducator, 1:1 Aide, Occupational Therapist)
- Auto-renewal, termination, and cancellation provisions
- Any "not-to-exceed" spending caps or annual expenditure limits
- Indemnification and insurance provisions

---

## Request 2: Itemized Invoices from Staffing Agencies

All invoices submitted to the district by the entities listed in Request 1 for **fiscal years 2022-23, 2023-24, and 2024-25**, itemized to include:

- Worker role or position title
- School site or department of placement
- Hours billed per pay period
- Bill rate per hour
- Total amount invoiced per billing cycle

---

## Request 3: Internal HR Vacancy and Recruitment Data

All internal reports, dashboards, summaries, or presentations produced by or for the Human Resources Department documenting:

- The number of **unfilled or vacant FTE positions** in Special Education, Speech-Language Pathology, School Nursing, and Paraeducator classifications for each school year from **2020-21 through 2024-25**
- **Time-to-fill** or **days-to-hire** metrics for certificated and classified positions during the same period
- Any internal correspondence, memos, board presentations, or reports regarding **recruitment difficulties**, **hard-to-fill positions**, or **critical shortage areas** during this period

---

## Request 4: Purchasing Authority and Delegation Documents

All currently effective and any previously amended versions of the following:

- **Board Policies 6210, 6220, and 6230** and their associated Administrative Procedures governing purchasing, competitive bidding, and contract approval
- Any **delegation of authority** documents, superintendent directives, or administrative orders authorizing the superintendent or designees to execute contracts for professional services or personal services without full board vote
- Any documentation of the **dollar thresholds** at which staff, the superintendent, and the full board are respectively required to approve a contract or purchase

---

## Request 5: Inter-fund Borrowing Resolutions

All board resolutions, staff reports, fiscal impact statements, and supporting documents authorizing **inter-fund loans or transfers** between the General Fund and any other district fund (including but not limited to the Capital Projects Fund, Transportation Vehicle Fund, and ASB Fund) for **fiscal years 2022-23, 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26**.

---

## Request 6: Annual Financial Statements (F-196)

The district's filed **F-196 Annual Financial Statements** as submitted to OSPI for **fiscal years 2020-21, 2021-22, 2022-23, and 2023-24**, showing actual expenditures by Object code for the General Fund.

If these reports are maintained only by OSPI rather than the district, please indicate that in your response so that I may redirect this portion of the request.

---

## General Provisions

1. **Format:** I request that all responsive records be provided in their native electronic format where possible. For documents that exist only in paper form, scanned PDF copies are acceptable.

2. **Fee Estimate:** If the estimated cost of fulfilling this request exceeds **$50.00**, please provide a written cost estimate before proceeding so that I may narrow the scope if necessary.

3. **Partial Production:** If any portion of this request can be fulfilled more quickly than other portions, I request that responsive records be produced on a rolling basis as they become available, rather than withholding all records until every request is complete.

4. **Exemptions:** If any records are withheld in whole or in part under a claimed exemption, please identify the specific statutory exemption relied upon for each withheld record and provide a brief explanation of how the exemption applies, as required by RCW 42.56.210(3).

5. **Acknowledgment:** Per RCW 42.56.520, I understand that the district must respond to this request within **five (5) business days** of receipt by either providing the records, providing a reasonable estimate of the time needed to respond, or denying the request with a stated reason.
