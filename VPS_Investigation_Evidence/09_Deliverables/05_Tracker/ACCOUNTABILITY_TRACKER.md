## How to Update This Tracker

When a records request is filed, update the "Filed?" column with the date. When a response arrives, note the date and summarize the response. When documents are received, note what was produced and what was withheld (with cited exemptions).

This tracker should be shared with anyone working on accountability for VPS vendor spending so that efforts are not duplicated and gaps are visible.
