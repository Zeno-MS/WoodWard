# CORPORATE VENDOR PROFILES

*This document outlines the private healthcare staffing agencies contracted by Vancouver Public Schools under Object 7 (Purchased Services). Payment totals represent deduplicated District Warrant Register data for the period of 2020-2025.*

---

## 1. Amergis Healthcare Staffing, Inc.
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $15,748,411 (48.9% of agency spend)
- **Headquarters:** Columbia, Maryland
- **Background:** Formerly Maxim Healthcare Staffing. Rebranded to Amergis in 2022 following corporate restructuring. One of the largest medical and educational staffing firms in the United States.
- **VPS Impact:** Largest single recipient of VPS staffing expenditures. In FY24-25 alone, Amergis extracted $10,970,973 from the district, representing over a quarter of all Object 7 real expenditures.
- **Regulatory/Legal:** [UNVERIFIED: Terms of specific VPS auto-renewal contracts]

## 2. Maxim Healthcare Services, Inc.
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $12,326,099 (38.3% of agency spend)
- **Headquarters:** Columbia, Maryland
- **Background:** Sister company/predecessor entity to Amergis Healthcare Staffing. 
- **VPS Impact:** Combined with Amergis, the Maxim corporate apparatus captured 87.2% ($28,074,510) of all VPS spending on staffing agencies during the analyzed period. 
- **Regulatory/Legal:** Reached a $150M settlement with the DOJ in 2011 to resolve criminal and civil charges related to fraudulent billing. Operating under agreements with VPS during peak austerity.

## 3. ProCare Therapy
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $2,926,334 (9.1% of agency spend)
- **Headquarters:** Atlanta, Georgia
- **Background:** A subsidiary of Stepping Stones Group / Jackson Healthcare. Specializes in school-based therapy and special education professionals (SLPs, RNs, School Psychologists).
- **VPS Impact:** Third largest vendor. Frequently fills hard-to-staff statutory roles.

## 4. Soliant Health, LLC
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $1,069,180 (3.3% of agency spend)
- **Headquarters:** Atlanta, Georgia (and Jacksonville, FL)
- **Background:** National healthcare staffing company focusing on schools, nursing, and allied health. Formerly part of Adecco Group, acquired by Olympus Partners in 2019.
- **VPS Impact:** Provides high-tier credentialed contractors.

## 5. Pioneer Healthcare Services, LLC
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $119,213 (0.4% of agency spend)
- **Headquarters:** San Diego, California
- **Background:** Specialized healthcare traveler staffing firm filling acute nursing and therapy shortages.
- **VPS Impact:** Minor vendor footprint relative to Amergis/Maxim, but still reflects the trend of outsourcing specialized care.

## 6. Aveanna Healthcare
- **VPS Payment Total (All-Time):** $695,200 (tracked separately; excluded from the canonical $32.2M primary-5-vendor total)
- **Headquarters:** Atlanta, Georgia
- **Background:** Major pediatric home health care company, frequently contracted by districts to provide 1-on-1 nursing for medically fragile students under IDEA/Section 504 mandates.
- **VPS Impact:** Payments appear across all fiscal years in the warrant register but are classified separately from the primary staffing agency vendors (Amergis, Maxim, Soliant, ProCare, Pioneer). Aveanna services are typically individualized student medical care rather than classroom staffing substitution.
