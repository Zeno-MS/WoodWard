# SOURCE DOCUMENT: VPS Board Policy 6220 (Bid or Proposal Requirements)

Board Policy 6220 establishes the district's competitive bidding framework. Crucially, it contains the "Single Source" and "Special Market" waivers used to authorize staffing contracts without public bidding.

### Section: Bid/Proposal Requirements (Waiver Provisions)
*"The district may waive bid requirements for purchases:*
1. *Clearly and legitimately limited to a single source of supply;*
2. *Involving special facilities or market conditions;*
3. *In the event of an emergency;*
4. *Of insurance or bonds; and*
5. *Involving public works in the event of an emergency."*

**Governance Note:** Between 2021 and 2025, the district revised Policy 6220 five separate times. In each revision, the "Professional Services" exemption was maintained, allowing staffing vendor expenditures to grow 38x (from $349K to $13.3M) without triggering a requirement for a competitive RFP process.

**Source:** VPS BoardDocs (Policy Archive); verified across five draft revisions (2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025).
